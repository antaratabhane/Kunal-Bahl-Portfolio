Professional Summary

As the Co-founder and CEO of Snapdeal, I have spent over a decade building one of India’s leading digital commerce platforms, serving millions of customers across the country. My journey began with a vision to democratize access to quality products for value-conscious consumers in Tier 2 and 3 cities. With a background in engineering from the University of Pennsylvania and business from The Wharton School, I have combined strategic thinking with operational execution to scale Snapdeal amidst one of the most dynamic markets in the world.

Beyond Snapdeal, I actively contribute to the startup ecosystem as an investor and mentor through Titan Capital, backing over 200 early-stage ventures. I am also part of several industry and government forums, advocating for digital innovation and entrepreneurship in India. My mission is to build inclusive, tech-driven solutions that create meaningful impact, foster economic opportunity, and inspire the next generation of Indian entrepreneurs.
